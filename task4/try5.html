What Inspired Us?

Protect and Celebrate the Bees

I always dreamt of opening a restaurant and while I was juggling to find a name for it, a notification popped on my mobile that said Bees are dying at an alarming rate. That's when I decided it has to be BitesBee - A Food Nest. Here's a fact: 90% of the world's nutrition is reliant on pollinators and Bees are heavily responsible for crop pollination. Without them, our ecosystem will collapse. So, here we are- protecting the bees and celebrating them by serving you home-like meals healthy, fresh and at pocket-friendly prices.

- Satish Saini
